## Laboratory Introduction

Our laboratory is dedicated to cutting-edge research and technology development in intelligent systems, image processing, signal processing, and multimodal electro-mechanical systems. Through interdisciplinary integration and industry-academia collaboration, we focus on research approaches that emphasize both theoretical innovation and practical applications, cultivating high-level technical talents and promoting academic development and technological advancement in related fields.

<table>
<tr>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

### Research Vision

Our laboratory primarily develops innovative technology solutions with practical value. Our research scope encompasses industrial intelligence, medical diagnostic technology, agricultural technology applications, and environmental monitoring systems.

</td>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

### Core Features

We are committed to applying advanced sensing technologies and intelligent algorithms to improve related industry efficiency and establish core competitiveness in cross-disciplinary technology integration.

</td>
</tr>
</table>

## Core Research Areas

<details>
<summary><b>Optical Detection and Image Processing Technology</b></summary>

<br>

| Technology Item | Core Technology | Application Domain |
|---------|----------|----------|
| **Precision Schlieren Optical System** | Non-contact micro-pollutant suspension capture technology | Dynamic analysis applications |
| **Multimodal Image Fusion Technology** | Airflow convection and temperature heterogeneous color image fusion algorithms | Defogging technology |
| **Color Schlieren Visualization System** | Real-time visualization of mask effects on respiratory airflow | Research platform development |
| **Night Vision Equipment Automatic Detection** | Intelligent image detection technology based | Automated night vision goggle detection system |

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Smart Manufacturing and Equipment Monitoring</b></summary>

<br>

| Technology Item | Core Technology | Application Domain |
|---------|----------|----------|
| **Machine Tool Intelligent Diagnosis Technology** | Based on Lyapunov exponent and machine learning | Grinding anomaly diagnosis system |
| **Thermal Imaging Real-time Imaging** | Dynamic thermal imaging real-time imaging system intelligent algorithms | Precise milling machine identification |
| **Vibration and Sound Signal Analysis** | Vibration and sound signal visualization imaging and feature analysis | Tool wear detection |
| **Automated System Integration** | UR robotic arm and spherical CNC lathe | Automatic loading and unloading system |

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Biomedical Signal Processing and Analysis</b></summary>

<br>

| Technology Item | Core Technology | Application Domain |
|---------|----------|----------|
| **Brainwave Signal Research** | Attention training for schizophrenia patients | Brain function and physiological signal correlation |
| **Multimodal Physiological Monitoring** | Integration of sensors and infrared thermal imaging | Human dynamic signal causal analysis |
| **Signal Visualization Technology** | Discrete wavelet transform and signal visualization technology | Disease correlation research |
| **Intelligent Analysis System** | Training precision and intelligent analysis | Biomedical signal processing platform |

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Agricultural Technology and Automation Applications</b></summary>

<br>

| Technology Item | Core Technology | Application Domain |
|---------|----------|----------|
| **Plant Disease Intelligent Detection** | Cross-modal image recognition technology | Malabar chestnut disease automated detection |
| **Agricultural Equipment Intelligence** | Automated equipment applications | Agricultural production efficiency improvement |
| **Precision Agriculture Technology** | Integration of multiple sensing technologies | Intelligent agricultural monitoring system |
| **Livestock Health Monitoring and Analysis** | Non-contact sensing technology | Intelligent livestock health management system |

</details>
